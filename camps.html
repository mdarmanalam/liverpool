Introduction to LFC Soccer Camps

The Language Gallery Juniors and Liverpool Football Club have teamed up to provide 12-17 year old boys and girls with the experience of a lifetime.

We offer an exciting summer programme that aims to both educate and entertain young people through a combination of language lessons and football coaching, giving them the chance to make friends and see a legendary part of football history.

Your children will be able to drastically improve their English language or sports leadership skills, mastering football techniques on the pitch. They’ll be under the guidance of the experienced coaches who train players for LFC and our expert language tutors, as well as being able to meet other children from all over the world.

For two weeks, students will enjoy a soccer camp packed with fun-filled sports, English lessons, and cultural activities to provide students with exciting experiences on each day of their stay. Our trips include scavenger hunts, FIFA tournaments, visits to iconic landmarks like Lancaster Castle, and Blackpool Pleasure Beach.

Our LFC Soccer Camps are a unique chance for young people to develop skills and friendships that will last them a lifetime.
